ingredients:
  - "3 ripe bananas"
  - "1/2 cup melted butter"
  - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
  - "1 cup all-purpose flour"
  - "1/2 cup cocoa powder"
  - "1 tsp baking soda"
  - "1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips"

instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)."
  - "Mash bananas in a bowl. Stir in melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla."
  - "Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda. Mix until smooth."
  - "Fold in 3/4 cup of chocolate chips."
  - "Pour into a greased loaf pan. Top with remaining chips."
  - "Bake for 50-60 minutes. Do not overbake to keep it fudgy!"

If you love brownies and banana bread, this is their love child. It's incredibly rich, moist, and chocolaty.

### Pro Tip
Use high-quality cocoa powder (Dutch-processed) for a darker color and smoother flavor.
